the importance

Sleep is essential for both physical and mental health. During sleep, the body repairs itself, strengthens the immune system, and processes memories. Teenagers, especially, need around 8–10 hours of sleep each night to function properly. Lack of sleep can lead to poor concentration, mood swings, and lower academic performance. Over time, it may also increase the risk of serious health problems. Building good habits—like avoiding screens before bed and maintaining a consistent sleep schedule—can greatly improve sleep quality.

The Rise of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming the world. From voice assistants to self-driving cars, AI is becoming part of everyday life. It helps businesses improve efficiency, doctors diagnose diseases, and students learn in new ways. However, AI also raises concerns about privacy and job loss. As technology continues to grow, it is important to use AI responsibly and ethically. Understanding AI today will help shape a better future tomorrow.
